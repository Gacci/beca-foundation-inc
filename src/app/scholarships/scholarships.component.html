Alice Newell Joslyn Medical Scholarship

$500 - $2,000

Eligibility

Requires an applicant to be entering the Medical/Health Care profession i.e.; dental/medical assistant, nursing, physical therapist, or seeking their Bachelor of Science, Master's or Doctorate in the health field.

Applicants must be living or attending college in San Diego County at the time of application. Eligibility is contingent upon a student's financial need, scholastic determination, and community/cultural awareness. Scholarship recipients may re-apply contingent on scholastic progress.
